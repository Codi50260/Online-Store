Codi de Faria

CEO / Founder

Codi was raised by adoptive parents in Cupertino, California, located in what is now known as Silicon Valley. Though he was interested in forensics, his passions of youth varied. He dropped out of Reed College, in Portland, Oregon, took a job at Atari Corporation as a video game designer in early 2015.

Back in Silicon Valley in the autumn of 2015, Codi reconnected with Stephen Wozniak, a former high school friend who was working for the Hewlett-Packard Company. When Wozniak told Codi of his progress in designing his own online store, Codi suggested that they go into business together, which they did. Cyber Oasis was built in Codi's family garage with money they obtained by selling NFT's and Wozniak's crypto.

Alexandra Valeria Botez

Marketing and Product Manager
